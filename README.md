# HabitHub

## Build Better Habits Together

HabitHub is a collaborative habit-tracking platform that helps users create, share, and maintain positive habits as part of a team.

Rather than building habits alone, users can join groups where members support and motivate one another. Team creators can set up habit challenges, invite members, and monitor collective progress. Each participant logs their daily activity, while the system tracks progress and displays leaderboards to encourage engagement and friendly competition.

By combining habit tracking, accountability, social interaction, and progress visualization, HabitHub helps users transform positive actions into sustainable long-term routines.

---

## Key Features

### Team Management

* Create and manage teams.
* Invite members using unique invite codes.
* Join existing teams through invitation codes.

### Habit Tracking

* Create and manage habits within teams.
* Log daily habit progress.
* Track completion history and consistency.

### Leaderboards

* View habit-specific leaderboards.
* Compare progress with other team members.
* Encourage healthy competition and accountability.

### Activity Logs

* View detailed logs of habit activity.
* Track personal progress over time.
* Monitor contributions from other team members.

### Reminders and Notifications

* Configure habit reminders.
* Receive notifications to stay on track.
* Reduce the chance of missing habit entries.

### Team Chat

* Communicate with team members through a built-in group chat.
* Share encouragement, updates, and achievements.
* Strengthen team collaboration and motivation.

### Session Management

* Monitor active sessions.
* Manage account security by terminating sessions when needed.

## Getting Started

### Creating Habits

To create and manage habits, you must first belong to a team.

You can do this in one of two ways:

#### 1. Create a Team

* Become a team creator.
* Invite other members.
* Create and manage habits for your team.

#### 2. Join a Team

* Obtain an invite code from a team creator.
* Enter the code in HabitHub.
* Gain immediate access to the team's habits and activities.

---

## Using HabitHub

Once you have joined a team, you can:

* Log your daily habit progress.
* View your team's leaderboard rankings.
* Track your personal statistics and completion rates.
* Review habit activity logs.
* Receive reminders and notifications.
* Communicate with teammates through the group chat.
